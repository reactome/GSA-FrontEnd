Welcome to Reactome Gene Set Analysis

Reactome GSA analysis is a powerful tool for understanding the biological significance of gene sets or pathways. It allows researchers to uncover the functional relevance of a list of genes in the context of biological pathways and processes.

Key Features:

  • Pathway Enrichment Analysis: Identifies Reactome pathways that are significantly enriched in your gene list.
  • Visualization Tools: Provides interactive visualizations to help you explore enriched pathways and their relationships.
  • Biological Context: Helps you understand how your genes contribute to specific biological processes.
  • Statistical Insights: Utilizes statistical methods to assess the significance of pathway enrichment.

How to Get Started:

  1. Upload Your Gene List: Input your list of genes or proteins of interest into the Reactome GSA tool.
  2. Run the Analysis: Start the analysis to identify enriched pathways and gain biological insights.
  3. Explore Results: Visualize and explore the results to understand the functional context of your gene list.
  4. Interpret Findings: Interpret the findings in the context of your research goals and biological questions.

Reactome GSA analysis is a valuable resource for researchers in genomics, systems biology, and bioinformatics. It can help you uncover the hidden biological connections within your gene sets and enhance your understanding of complex biological systems.
